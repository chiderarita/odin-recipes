Coconut Soup

Description

This is a soup recipe used with coconut water

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Sait
  • Coconut water
  • spices

Steps

  1. Put in your coconut water in the pot
  2. Add your meat and spices inside
  3. allow it to boil for some time
  4. tast to see what is missing
  5. remove after all are done cooking
