Beef Stew

Description

This is a stew recipe used for making beef stew

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Sait
  • Beef meat
  • spices

Steps

  1. Add water in the pot
  2. Add your beef meat and spices inside
  3. allow it to boil for some time
  4. tast to see what is missing
  5. remove after all are done cooking
