Pork Stew

Description

This is a stew recipe used with a pork meat and potatoes for it sweetness

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Sait
  • Pork meat
  • Potatoes
  • spices

Steps

  1. Put water in the pot
  2. Add your pork meat and spices inside
  3. allow it to boil for some time
  4. put your potatoes and turn it
  5. tast to see what is missing
  6. remove after all are done cooking
